YARGODYNE NLP Research Task Force Targets

As tasked, the team has developed infrastructure for the generation and evaluation of NLP models:

  • Latest stand-alone, high compression, high performance NGram algorithm called Knesser-Ney smoothing, as implemented by Keneth Heafield.

  • This allowed us to choose the smallest possible yet performant set of n-grams to run in our constrained production environment.

  • Considerable time was spent in building a thoroughly comprehensive and scalable analysis pipeline, this will enable us to update our model as the app is used.
